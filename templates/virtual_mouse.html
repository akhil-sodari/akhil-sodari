Virtual Mouse System Addressing the Carpal Tunnel Syndrome

Akhil Sodari


Hey, how are you doing today? This was one of the very first projects I have ever done. It is my minor project during my Bachelor's in Computer Science and Engineering at Mahatma Gandhi Institute of Technology. 


From my childhood, I was always fascinated by sci-fi movies, and especially Iron Man 2008, where the protagonist operates the display without touching it.

I have seen that in many movies afterwards, and I used to get excited everytime I watched such sort of tech. So, for my minor project I wanted to do something similar and showcase to the Class of 2021. But just doing a normal tech with a reference From movie doesn't make any sense, So I wanted to add a bit of usefullness to it. 

I am a person who always work to build something useful for the world, Therefore I did some research and found out about the Carpal Tunnel Syndrome, and I thought I could apply my technology to address this issue. Therefore, I build this project around carpal tunnel syndrome. Ideally, I want to explain the project in this post, but I have already prepared a good documentation. So, I am attaching it with this email and you can go through it.
