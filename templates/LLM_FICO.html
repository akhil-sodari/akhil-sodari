Leveraging LLMs and ChatGPT for Enhanced Decision Making in the Financial Industry – Fair Issac Corporation (FICO)

Akhil Sodari


 • Conducted comprehensive research on FICO and its role in the credit risk assessment industry and decision management solutions.

 • Collaborated with the project team to assess LLM-based systems accuracy and efficiency in financial applications.

 • Explored competitors' adoption of AI technologies in their products, contributing to the development of an industry-wide framework. 

• Designed an industry wide framework for companies in the financial industry to assess the viability of leveraging LLMs in their business.

 • Developed a Python Flask Application based on the designed framework and integrated it with ChatGPT API, enhancing dynamic usability. 

• Presented findings and deliverables to FICO executives; framework and prototype were well-received for their strategic value.
